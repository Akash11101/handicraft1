Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your common questions.

General Questions

What is Artisanal Crafts?

Artisanal Crafts is an online marketplace dedicated to showcasing and selling handcrafted products from skilled artisans around the world. We aim to preserve traditional craftsmanship and provide a platform for artisans to reach a global audience.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also view your order status and tracking information in your My Account section if you have an account with us.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. You can see the estimated shipping cost during the checkout process.

Product & Craftsmanship

Are all products genuinely handmade?

Absolutely! We carefully vet our artisans to ensure that all products sold on our platform are genuinely handcrafted using traditional techniques and sustainable practices wherever possible. We believe in the beauty and uniqueness of handmade items.

How do I care for my handmade items?

Care instructions vary depending on the material and craft. Each product page includes specific care recommendations. Generally, handmade items require gentle care to preserve their integrity and beauty. If you have specific questions, feel free to contact us.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items, starting from the day you receive your order. Items must be in their original condition. Please refer to our Terms of Service for full details on eligibility and the return process.
